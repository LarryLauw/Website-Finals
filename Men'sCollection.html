Types of Men's Shoes

1. Sneakers

Sneakers are comfortable shoes suitable for everyday activities. They come in various trendy designs and colors.

2. Formal Shoes

Formal shoes are suitable for formal events or the office. They are typically made of leather and have an elegant design.

3. Boots

Boots provide a masculine look and are suitable for cold weather. They are available in various styles, ranging from ankle boots to knee-high boots.
