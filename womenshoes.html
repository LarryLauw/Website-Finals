Types of Women's Shoes

1. High Heels

High heels add elegance and height to a woman's silhouette. They come in various styles, including stilettos, pumps, and wedges.

2. Sneakers

Sneakers are comfortable and versatile, perfect for casual outings or activities. They come in a wide range of colors and designs.

3. Ballet Flats

Ballet flats are stylish and comfortable flat shoes, suitable for both casual and semi-formal occasions. They often have a rounded toe and a simple design.
